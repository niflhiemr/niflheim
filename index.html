《Life Of Pi》

Life is so beautiful that death has fallen in love with it

生命太美了 死亡爱上了它

a jealous, possessive love that grabs at when it can

这是一种充满了嫉妒心和占有欲的爱 它紧紧抓住所能抓到的一切

But life leaps over oblivion lightly, losing only a thing or two of no importance

但是生命轻盈地跃过死亡，只失去了一两样不重要的东西

and gloom is but the passing shadow of a cloud.

沮丧只是云朵飘过时投下的阴影 很快便消失了
